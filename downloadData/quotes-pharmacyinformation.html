Rx Choice Tiered Network (Includes Retail90 and Rx Maintenance 90)

With the Rx Choice Tiered Network, you can choose from two levels of coverage, helping to provide both savings and access:

► Level 1: You can get prescriptions filled at Level 1 pharmacies for the same copay or percentage of the drug costs you usually pay as part of your prescription drug plan.

► Level 2: For an extra copay or percentage of the drug cost (depending on your benefit), you have more choices with Level 2 pharmacies, no matter which drug you fill.

The National Network varies by state and may apply if you get your health insurance plan from an employer, if you purchase your coverage yourself, or if you have certain grandfathered plans. The Rx Choice Tiered Network may not be available in all states. The Rx Choice Tiered Network may apply if:

    • You get your health insurance plan from an employer or if you have certain grandfathered plans.
    • You purchased a plan on your state or federal Health Insurance Marketplace (also known as the exchange)
    • You purchased coverage off the exchange and not through your employer
    • Your coverage is through a Small Group employer on, and in some cases, off the exchange
